Why You Should Visit Wilpattu

Historical Marvels

Wilpattu National Park boasts ancient ruins and religious places, adding to its historical significance. Explore the park to discover hidden archeological treasures and learn about Sri Lanka's rich history.

Serene Safari Experience

The park's calm setting and diverse species provide an amazing safari experience.Wilpattu is less congested than other parks, providing a more quiet safari experience.

Unique Landscape

The park's scenery has natural lakes, known as "Willus," that support a healthy ecology and provide a peaceful habitat for wildlife and people.
